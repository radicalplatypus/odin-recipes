Tea

Description

Tea is sweet.

You should obviously drink tea.

Ingredients

  • Tea
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Place tea in a cup
  3. Add boiled water to tea
  4. Steep for 1 minute
  5. Add milk and/or sugar to taste
  6. Drink the tea
