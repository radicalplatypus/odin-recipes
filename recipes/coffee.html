Coffee

Description

Coffee is sweet.

You should obviously drink coffee.

Ingredients

  • Ground coffee
  • Water
  • Milk or cream
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Grind the coffee
  2. Place ground coffee in French Press
  3. Add water
  4. Brew 4 minutes
  5. Add milk, cream and/or sugar to taste.
